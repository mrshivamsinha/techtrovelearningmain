About

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Techtrove Learning embarked on its journey in the realm of education and innovation in 2009, marking the humble beginnings of a vision to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital age. Over the next two years, the institute laid its foundation, diligently crafting its identity and purpose.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    In March 2011, Techtrove Learning took a significant step forward as it transformed from a modest endeavor into a full-fledged agency. This pivotal moment marked the official birth of an institution dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions in various fields, including web development, soft skills training, and SolidWorks.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    By December 2012, Techtrove Learning had evolved into a dynamic entity, transitioning into a full-service institute offering a wide array of courses and training programs. This strategic move expanded its reach and capabilities, enabling it to cater to a diverse audience with varied learning needs and aspirations.


  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    Building on its success and growing demand, Techtrove Learning entered a new phase of expansion in July 2014. This phase marked a significant milestone as the institute expanded its facilities, course offerings, and outreach efforts. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, Techtrove Learning continued to push boundaries and empower individuals to unlock their full potential in an ever-changing world.
